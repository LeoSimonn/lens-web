Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 8, 2025

Introduction

Lens ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Data Collection

Lens is designed to be privacy-first. We do not require you to create an account to use the application.

  • Photos: Photos you take with Lens are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your photos to any server.
  • Camera & Microphone: The app requires access to your camera to take photos. This data is processed in real-time on your device and is not collected by us.

Analytics

We may use anonymous usage data to improve app performance and stability (e.g., crash reports). This data does not include personal information or your photos.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@lens.app

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reveal the photo?

Just like a real instant camera! Shake your phone gently to speed up the development process. You'll feel haptic feedback as you shake.

Where do my photos go?

Once developed, your photos are automatically saved to your standard iOS Photo Library.

The app is stuck printing?

This is a rare issue. Please force close the app and restart it. If it persists, contact us.
